There’s a legal process for referring serious misconduct by a teacher in England.

You’ll need to follow these steps:

  • Report your concerns locally
  • Check if TRA will deal with the allegation
  • Report the teacher to TRA using this service (online or using the paper form)
  • Wait to hear back from TRA
  • If TRA decide to investigate, they’ll send a copy of the allegations to the teacher
  • TRA will decide whether there is a case to answer
  • A professional conduct panel meeting or hearing takes place
  • TRA confirm the outcome of the meeting or hearing

Read more on what happens after you’ve referred a teacher.
