The Teaching Regulation Agency (TRA) will not deal with cases relating to minor misconduct, or a teacher’s performance or competence.

You should:

  1. talk to the school or sixth-form college first to try to solve the problem.
  2. contact the school’s board of governors or contact the council if you’re not happy with the response.

You’ll need to provide proof to support your complaint.
