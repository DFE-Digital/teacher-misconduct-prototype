What happens next

Once the Teaching Regulation Agency (TRA) have reviewed the report, they’ll let you know what happens next.

TRA could tell you they:

  • need more information to decide whether to investigate the case
  • will not investigate the case because it’s outside their remit
  • will not investigate the case because it does not have the potential to result in a prohibition order
  • will launch a formal investigation

The time it takes for TRA to get back to you will depend on the case.

For more information about what happens next, see our step-by-step guide.
